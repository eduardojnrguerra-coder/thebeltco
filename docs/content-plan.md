# Content Plan — First 10 SEO Articles

> Guide pages, buying advice, and style content designed to capture search traffic, build authority, and drive product discovery.

---

## Content Rules

- Every article is a **Shopify blog post** under a single blog called "Guides" (handle: `/blogs/guides/`)
- Every article must pass the **read-aloud test** — sound human, not AI-generated
- Every article must include **internal links** to: 1 collection, 1 related guide, 1 product or best-seller
- Every article must include a **FAQ section** with 3–5 questions
- No fake claims — only state what can be substantiated
- Minimum 800 words, maximum 1500 words
- Publish 1 per week for 10 weeks, then 1–2 per month ongoing
